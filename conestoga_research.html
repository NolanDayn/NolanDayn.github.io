Disenfector

During a Co-op with Conestoga Research I worked on a team with Engineering Students where we did a redesign of an industry partners device for disinfecting cars, trucks, busses and other vehicles from hazards such as Covid-19. I was the electronics and software member of the team and responsible for changes to the devices embedded software and electrical/electronic systems.

I wont give away much technical information to respect the confidentiality of the design. But we did make several changes to the systems structure and performance. I did work with a wide variety of sensors, actuators and communication protocols. It was a great project and a very rewarding experience. I had a good time working with my fellow team members and everyone at Conestoga Research.
